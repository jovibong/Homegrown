About HomeGrown

Upskilling Competencies | Managing Finances | Building Communities

Homegrown is a web-based platform designed to empower foreign workers in Singapore through upskilling, financial management, and community support. Our goal is to provide workers with tailored educational resources, easy-to-use budgeting tools, and opportunities to connect with mentors and fellow workers. By fostering a sense of community and offering practical solutions, Homegrown aims to make life in Singapore smoother and more fulfilling for foreign workers.
